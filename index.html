What is the Marvel Cinematic Universe

The Marvel Cinematic Universe is a collection of television series and movies that show us the narrative behind the MArvel characters and their stories. In relation to the MCU, Disney and Marvel bring heroes to life from Spider-Man and Iron to Black Panther and Doctor Strange.

In recent years we have seen many films from the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) breaking records and they can carry that praise from critics.

The story we see on the big screeen started when Tony Stark became Iron Man. If you have not yet seen this film then I am sorry but it is not to late to get the time to watch it. But without spoiling anything Tony Stark became aware of problems that occur all around the world and took initative to resolve them with his knowledge. As time goes we are introduced to other heroes such as Thor and Captain America. This later ties in to creating the Avengers Initiative which is a plan by Nick Furry, an agent of SHIELD, to bring the greatest and mightiest heroes of the world to defend Earth from big threats.
