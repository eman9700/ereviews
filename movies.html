My Top 7 Marvel Movies

The Best-Reviewed Marvel Movies, According to Me

In recent years we have seen many films from the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) breaking records and they can carry that praise from critics.

Here you will find the best movies in my opinion.

Top 7 MARVEL MOVIES

I have to admit ranking only 7 marvel movies is pretty hard. The simple fact is that I enjoy every single one no matter what the world says. They are all good to me. But if I were to choose well here it goes.

Number 7: Spider-man 2

I know I know. This is wasn't really part of the actual MCU but c'mon NOSTALGIA.I personally would say that the whole trilogy is pretty tied but out of all of them this one, oh man, is the one that stands out the most.

Number 6: Avengers Age of Ultron

I think this should be up here just the simple fact that it shows a bit of weakness in our heroes. They were really put to the test fighting against Tony's own creation. But then introding a lot of important charcters that are still seen to this day.

Number 5: Doctor Strange

He is the sourcer supreme. Like he is literal magic. He is so powerful that I can't even put it into words. And Benedict just fits the character so perfectly.

Number 4: Shange Chi

I reallt enjoyed this movies because I like anime and I feel like they kind of incorporated it because anime is so big right now. They executed it very well and it brought more diversity into the MCU.

Number 3: Black Panther

My respects to Chadwick. BE played the Black Panther very well and I just can't see myself seeing another actor taking his place. I know they were films coming out for his character and a lot of things had to change throughout the MCU just because of what occurred.

Number 2: Avengers Endgame

Endgame has to be up here it literally broke records. It brought so much excitement. I was literally crying and yelling and all kinds of stuff when I was at the theatre. Best movie ever.

Number 1: Spider-man No Way Home

Yes, this is my number 1. I liked it but I kind of expected more. I mainly just loved the fact that productions heard the voices of the fans and integrated all 3 actors that have played Spider-Man.
